Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our web development process.

Q What type of websites do you create?

We create both WordPress websites and custom coded websites according to your business needs. Whether you need a business website, portfolio, eCommerce store, or landing page, we can build a professional and responsive website for you.

Q Which is better: WordPress or Custom Coded Website?

WordPress websites are best for fast, affordable, and easy-to-manage websites. Custom coded websites are better for unique designs, advanced features, high performance, and fully customized functionality. We help you choose the best option for your business.

Q How long does it take to complete a website?

The delivery time depends on the project size and requirements. A basic website usually takes 1 day, while advanced or custom coded websites may take 5-9 days.

Q Will my website be mobile-friendly and SEO optimized?

Yes. All websites we create are fully responsive for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices. We also follow basic SEO practices to help your website perform better on search engines like Google.

Q Do you provide support after the website is completed?

Yes, we provide after-sale support and assistance. If you need updates, maintenance, bug fixes, or guidance related to your website, our team is available to help you.
